The Bicycle Test

(A semi-serious movie test)

TL;DR

The Bicycle Test is passed by a movie if it satisfies one condition:

there must exist at least one scene in which a named character rides a bike.

Some time ago i discovered the Bechdel test, a test born as a joke about the representation of women in fiction.

I’ve found it pretty terrifying, as even with those banal conditions lots of movies doesn’t pass it.

On the other side, in spite of the climate crisis, in fiction private cars represents pretty much the only accepted way of moving around.

This test do exists for answering the question: is in some way an alternative mobility represented?
